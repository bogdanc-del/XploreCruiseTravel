  - Hero banner: "8,400+ cruises worldwide"
  - Guided Recommendation banner (persistent nudge)
  - Sticky filter bar: search, sort, filters toggle
  - Expandable filter panel: destination, cruise line, type, date, price, nights
  - Cruise grid: 3 columns, 24 items/page, grouped departures
  - Pagination controls
  - Floating guided CTA (appears when banner scrolls away)

  - Hero with gallery or single image, title, badges
  - Tabbed content: Overview, Itinerary, Cabins, Included, Beverages, Cancellation, Terms
  - Sidebar (sticky): price card, A/B-tested CTAs, departure date picker, summary
  - "Need Help?" card (phone + email)
  - Advisor Note (consultant photo + personalized note)
  - Similar Cruises (3 cards)
  - Port Drawer (slide-out on port click)
  - Chat Widget

Step 4: LEAD CAPTURE FORM (Modal overlay on Detail Page)
  - Cruise context card (title + price)
  - 4 fields: Name, Email, Phone (required), Message
  - GDPR checkbox
  - Submit -> notifyNewLead() email to consultant
  - Success state: generic "we'll get back to you"

Step 5: CONSULTANT EMAIL & FOLLOW-UP
  - Email notification via Nodemailer/Brevo
  - Consultant responds manually with personalized offer
  - Average response target: not communicated to user

CONVERSION TARGETS (from Business Analysis):
  Visit -> Browse:           >60% engagement
  Browse -> Detail:          >25% CTR
  Detail -> Form Open:       >15% CTA click rate
  Form Open -> Submit:       >50% completion
  Lead -> Sale:              >20% consultant conversion
  Overall Visit -> Sale:     >1.5%

2. PAIN POINTS
================================================================================

--- FRICTION POINTS ---

F1. NO HEADER CTA
    The top navigation has 4 links (Home, Cruises, About, Contact) but no
    conversion button. Every travel OTA puts a "Book/Call/Quote" CTA in the
    persistent header. Users must scroll to find conversion paths.
    Impact: Missed micro-conversions from users ready to act at any moment.

F2. FILTERS HIDDEN BY DEFAULT (Listing Page)
    The filter panel is collapsed behind a toggle button. Desktop users expect
    visible filters (sidebar or always-open top bar). Extra click = extra friction.
    Impact: Users who want to refine searches may not discover filters exist.

F3. DUAL COMPETING CTAs ON CRUISE CARDS
    Each CruiseCard has "View Details" AND "Request Offer". Both go to the
    same detail page (one with ?offer=1). Two buttons of similar size create
    decision paralysis and dilute the primary action.
    Impact: Lower click-through; unclear which action the brand wants users to take.

F4. PHONE NUMBER REQUIRED IN LEAD FORM
    All 3 fields (name, email, phone) are required. Phone is high-friction for
    early-funnel users who haven't committed. Cruise consideration cycles are
    long; users may abandon rather than share phone numbers.
    Impact: Estimated 15-25% form abandonment from phone requirement alone.

F5. NO EXPECTED RESPONSE TIME IN SUCCESS STATE
    After submitting the lead form, the success message says "we'll get back to
    you" with no timeframe. Users don't know if they'll hear back in 1 hour or
    1 week. Uncertainty erodes trust.
    Impact: Higher anxiety, potential duplicate submissions, lost trust.

F6. 7 TABS ON DETAIL PAGE = COGNITIVE OVERLOAD
    Overview, Itinerary, Cabins, Included/Excluded, Beverages, Cancellation,
    Terms. On mobile, tabs overflow horizontally. Users may never discover
    Cabins or Cancellation info.
    Impact: Key decision-making information (cancellation, inclusions) is buried.

--- TRUST GAPS ---

T1. NO TESTIMONIALS/REVIEWS ON DETAIL PAGE
    Both TestimonialsSection and ReviewsSection components exist with "detail"
    variants ready, but they are NOT rendered on the cruise detail page.
    Social proof is only on the homepage -- absent at the conversion point.
    Impact: Critical miss. Social proof at the decision point can lift
    conversions by 10-15%.

T2. NO AGGREGATE RATING VISIBLE ANYWHERE
    Individual review cards show star ratings, but there's no "4.8/5 from 200
    reviews" aggregate badge on the homepage or detail page.
    Impact: Aggregate ratings are processed faster than individual reviews
    and provide immediate trust signals.

T3. NO URGENCY OR SCARCITY BEYOND CTA TEXT
    Only A/B variant B says "Locuri limitate" (Limited spots). No countdown
    timers, no "X spots left" indicators, no "Y people viewing this" signals.
    Impact: High-consideration purchases need urgency triggers. Without them,
    users defer decisions indefinitely.

T4. NO PHONE NUMBER IN HEADER
    Cruise buyers (30-65 demographic) value phone access. The phone number
    is only in the footer and the detail page "Need Help?" card.
    Impact: Users seeking reassurance through human contact bounce earlier.

--- MISSING ENGAGEMENT POINTS ---

E1. NO EMAIL CAPTURE / NEWSLETTER
    Visitors who leave without contacting have zero re-engagement path. No
    newsletter signup, no "email me price alerts", no "save this cruise".
    Impact: Long consideration cycles (2-6 weeks for cruise purchases) mean
    most first-time visitors leave and never return.

E2. NO WISHLIST/SAVE FEATURE
    Users can't bookmark cruises for later comparison. The only "next step" is
    contacting the consultant. For a product with 8,400+ options, this creates
    a single-session-or-nothing dynamic.
    Impact: Higher bounce rate, lower return visits, lost consideration-stage leads.

E3. NO MOBILE STICKY CTA ON DETAIL PAGE
    On mobile, the sidebar with price/CTAs renders below the main content.
    Users must scroll past 7 tabs of content to find the conversion action.
    Impact: Mobile users (typically 60-70% of traffic) have severely degraded
    conversion paths.

E4. NO PRICE ANCHORING ON HOMEPAGE HERO
    The hero section has a headline and "Explore Cruises" button but no price
    hook. "Cruises from 299 EUR" would immediately qualify interest and reduce
    bounces from users who assume cruises are unaffordable.
    Impact: Missed opportunity to set expectations and reduce bounce rate.

3. PM PROPOSED JOURNEY
================================================================================

PERSPECTIVE: Maximize qualified lead volume, reduce friction, optimize funnel metrics.

PROPOSED CHANGES:

PM-1. ADD PERSISTENT HEADER CTA
      Place a "Solicita Oferta" / "Get a Quote" button in the navbar,
      visible on all pages. On mobile, use a phone icon button.
      Expected impact: +8-12% increase in lead form opens from non-detail pages.

PM-2. MAKE PHONE OPTIONAL IN LEAD FORM
      Change phone from required to optional. Add micro-copy:
      "Optional -- helps us reach you faster."
      Expected impact: +15-20% increase in form completion rate.

PM-3. SINGLE PRIMARY CTA ON CRUISE CARDS
      Remove "Request Offer" button from CruiseCard. Keep "View Details" as
      the sole CTA. The detail page is where conversion happens.
      Expected impact: +5-8% increase in card CTR (less decision paralysis).

PM-4. ADD RESPONSE TIME PROMISE TO SUCCESS STATE
      "We'll respond within 2 hours during business hours (Mon-Fri, 9-18)."
      Expected impact: +10% increase in form trust, fewer duplicate submissions.

PM-5. ADD SOCIAL PROOF TO DETAIL PAGE
      Render TestimonialsSection (detail variant) and ReviewsSection
      below the main content on the cruise detail page.
      Expected impact: +10-15% lift in lead form opens.

PM-6. ADD URGENCY INDICATORS TO DETAIL PAGE SIDEBAR
      Show departure countdown: "Plecare in X zile" (Departing in X days).
      Show cabin scarcity when data available.
      Expected impact: +5-10% lift in immediate form submissions.

PM-7. ADD MOBILE STICKY CTA BAR
      Fixed bottom bar on mobile with price summary + "Solicita Oferta" button.
      Appears after scrolling past the hero.
      Expected impact: +20-30% lift in mobile lead form opens.

PM-8. SHOW FILTERS BY DEFAULT ON DESKTOP LISTING
      Remove the toggle. Show filter panel open on desktop (>=1024px).
      Keep toggle behavior on mobile.
      Expected impact: +10-15% increase in filter usage, better qualified browsing.

4. SERVICE DESIGNER PROPOSED JOURNEY
================================================================================

PERSPECTIVE: Emotional journey, progressive trust, cognitive comfort, delight moments.

PROPOSED CHANGES:

SD-1. PROGRESSIVE TRUST BUILDING ON DETAIL PAGE
      Layer trust signals throughout the page, not just at bottom:
      - Consultant presence: Add advisor note ABOVE the tabs, not below.
      - Social proof: Show testimonials between content sections.
      - Guarantee language: "Fara costuri ascunse" (No hidden costs) near price.
      Expected impact: Reduced bounce from detail page, longer time-on-page.

SD-2. REDUCE TAB COGNITIVE LOAD
      Consolidate 7 tabs into 4:
      - "Prezentare" (Overview + Ship + Included/Excluded)
      - "Itinerariu" (Itinerary + Map)
      - "Cabine & Preturi" (Cabins + Pricing)
      - "Conditii" (Cancellation + Terms + Beverages)
      Expected impact: Higher discovery of important info (cancellation, cabins).

SD-3. EMOTIONAL PRICE ANCHORING
      Instead of "from EUR 899 /person", reframe as:
      "from EUR 128/night -- your floating hotel, meals, entertainment included."
      Add micro-copy: "All-inclusive value" under price.
      Expected impact: Reframes price perception from expensive to value.

SD-4. WARM SUCCESS EXPERIENCE
      After lead form submit, instead of a generic success message:
      - Show consultant photo (Daniela) with a warm message
      - "Daniela va reveni cu o oferta personalizata in maxim 2 ore."
      - Show 3 things that will happen next: 1) Confirmation email, 2) Personalized
        offer, 3) No-obligation conversation.
      Expected impact: Reduces post-submission anxiety, builds anticipation.

SD-5. LISTING PAGE AS DISCOVERY EXPERIENCE
      Add "quick preview" hover cards on cruise listings showing:
      - Top 3 ports of call
      - Key inclusion (e.g., "All meals included")
      - One-line hook ("New ship, launched 2024")
      Without requiring a click to the detail page.
      Expected impact: Higher engagement, more informed clicks.

SD-6. CONSISTENT CTA LANGUAGE
      Current: "Solicita oferta" / "Verifica disponibilitatea" / "Obtine pret" /
      "Vorbeste cu un expert" (4+ different CTA messages).
      Proposed: Standardize to max 2 messages across the funnel:
      - Primary: "Solicita oferta gratuita" (Request free offer)
      - Secondary: "Suna acum" (Call now)
      Expected impact: Reduced confusion, stronger brand voice.
